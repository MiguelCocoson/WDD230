Target Audiance

This website focus on people with small bussiness, so they can have the opportunity to be noticed by the community by been present in events and collaborating with the city, while helping the locals find and learn about the local business and events that are taking place in their city.

Personas

  1. People with small businesses.
  2. People that recently moved to the city.

Scenarios

  1. What events are occuring in the area?
  2. What recreational activities can I do with my family in the city?
  3. What events can my business can be part of?
  4. What can I do to be involve in the community?
  5. What services are available in the city?
