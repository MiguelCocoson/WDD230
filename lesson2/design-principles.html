Hick's Law

Apple

www.apple.com

I think this website follows Hick's Law because, unlike most companies, Apple have a very small list of products, making the website be populated by a very small amount of options for the customer.

Fitt's Law

Youtube

www.youtube.com

I think this website follows Fitt's Law because every time that I am on the website, either from my computer or from my phone, I feel like if most things are positioned and have the right size to click on them with just a simple mouse movement, specially the recomendation list at the right size of the playing video.

White Space and Clean Design

Google

www.amazon.com

Specially on a desktop screen, anyone can notice how clean and simple is the main page of Google. It is just the search bar and a few more buttons.
